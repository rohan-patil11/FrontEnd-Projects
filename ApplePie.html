Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Mix apples, sugars, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour in a bowl. Place one crust in a pie dish and fill with apple mixture. Dot with butter and cover with the second crust. Crimp edges and make slits on top. Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown. Cool before serving.
