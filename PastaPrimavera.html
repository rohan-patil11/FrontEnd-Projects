Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup yellow squash, sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

Cook pasta according to package instructions. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add cherry tomatoes, zucchini, and squash. Cook for 5-7 minutes until tender. Toss with cooked pasta, Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
