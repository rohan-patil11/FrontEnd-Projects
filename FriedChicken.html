Fried Chicken

Ingredients :

  • Chicken – Drumsticks and chicken thighs are the best though you can use any other part like chicken wings or chicken breast to fry.
  • Buttermilk – Crucial for soaking the chicken prior to frying to tenderize the meat.
  • Hot sauce – Adds a nice spice to your buttermilk mixture.
  • Flour and cornstarch – Create the signature crispy texture.
  • Seasonings – Garlic powder, oregano, white pepper, basil, and smoked paprika for perfect flavor.

Recipe :

While the time on this recipe is over 4 hours, that’s mainly because you’ve got to let it sit in buttermilk to tenderize for juicy meat inside. After that, this recipe is simple! Shake the coating on and fry it up to perfection. Crispy fried chicken from scratch is so easy. Make the buttermilk mixture, soak the chicken in the fridge, coat it until well seasoned, and deep fry it. Yum!
