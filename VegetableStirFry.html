Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe:

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant. Toss in broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.
